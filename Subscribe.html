Why Textbook Web Was Created

Textbook Web was created with one simple mission: to help students access affordable, high-quality academic resources without breaking the bank. We understand the financial strain that textbooks can put on students—often costing hundreds, even thousands, of rands for books you'll only need for a single semester.

Instead of spending up to R1000 or more on a textbook that might just gather dust after the semester ends, Textbook Web offers a more sustainable solution. For as little as R40 a month, you can subscribe to access the textbooks you need, saving you money while ensuring you have the resources required to succeed in your studies.

This subscription model means you can spend as little as R200 for all the textbooks you need in a semester—significantly cheaper than buying new or even second-hand books. Textbook Web gives you the freedom to choose the resources that suit your course load, without the heavy financial burden of purchasing physical books.

Our platform aims to empower students by offering a vast library of open-access textbooks and expert summaries, ensuring that learning is affordable, convenient, and accessible to everyone. Whether you're a first-year student or a postgraduate scholar, Textbook Web is here to make your academic journey more affordable and efficient.
